Our Services

Feature Films

A feature film is a film that's long enough to be considered a "main" attraction. The film must be longer than 40 minutes to be considered a feature film.

Web Series

It’s a show which is only available to watch on the internet via OTT platforms such as Netflix,Amazon prime video which you have to pay for.

Shortfilms

It is any motion picture not long enough in running time to be considered a feature film. It is an original motion picture that has a running time of 40 minutes or less, including all credits.

Cover Songs

In popular music, a cover version, remake, cover song, revival, or simply cover, is a new performance or recording by someone other than the original artist or composer of a previously recorded song.

Documentaries

A documentary film is a non-fictional, motion picture intended to "document reality, primarily for the purposes of instruction or maintaining a historical record".

Ad Films

It is a span of television programming produced and paid for by an organization. It conveys a message promoting, and aiming to market, a product or service.
